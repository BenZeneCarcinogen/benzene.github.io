What we offer

At New, Clear and Poor Records, we provide a platform for bands to thrive.
We celebrate the spirit of independence in music.
We offer free recording, mixing and mastering sessions, working with bands so that they sound like the terminal retards that they aspire to.
We provide help with designing & creating Merch and promotional material.
We endeavour to help bands get gigs by including those that we love on line-ups and putting them in touch with relevant promoters.
